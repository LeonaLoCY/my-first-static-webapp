WHY ASSURITY?
As the only National Certification Authority (NCA) in Singapore, we want to ensure Singaporeans feel safe accessing online services.

Join us and you’ll be part of a team that conceptualises and contributes to new ways of living, powered by technology.

Your work will also help generate growth opportunities for businesses that can now utilise strong authentication to roll out innovative online services.
