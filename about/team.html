Mr Robert Chew
Chairman, Assurity Trusted Solutions Pte Ltd.
Robert was formerly a partner of Accenture where he led the strategy practice in Southeast Asia and the Communications, Media and High-Tech industry practice in Singapore. Since his retirement in 2007, he has remained active in the technology industry as Managing Partner of early stage technology investors, iGlobe Partners and Stream Global.

Robert is an active angel investor in the United States, and is the Singapore ambassador for Singularity University, based in Mountain View, California, and a member of the Innovation Board of XPRIZE Foundation.

He is currently a board member of National Healthcare Group, Singapore University of Technology and Design, National Council of Social Service, Anacle Pte. Ltd., StaffonDemand Pte Ltd, Kwong Wai Shiu Hospital, SourceSage Pte Ltd, iGlobe Partners (II) Pte Ltd, Stream Global Pte Ltd, Singapore Mediation Centre, Shared Services for Charities Ltd and Tan Kah Kee Foundation.

He is a Fellow of the Singapore Computer Society (SCS) and was awarded SCS's IT Leader of the Year in 2009, and SCS's President Award in 2014. He chaired the Information Technology Standards Committee from 2005 to 2012, and currently chairs its Cloud Computing Standards Task Force. For his contribution to standards development, he was awarded SPRING's Distinguished Partner Award in 2011.

He was conferred the Public Service Medal in 2013 and the Public Service Star in 2017.

Ms Ang Mui Kim
Board Director/Chief Executive Officer
A recipient of the Public Administration Medal (Silver) National Day Award 2020 and listed in the inaugural Singapore 100 Women in Tech in 2020, Mui Kim has been instrumental in bringing about business innovation and transformation in both the private and public sectors.

Mui Kim started her career with the National Computer Board as a programmer. Prior to joining GovTech in 2009, she was the Group General Manager at SingTel’s subsidiary NCS responsible for a business unit that grew successfully locally and in key regional markets such as Australia, Malaysia and China.

Mui Kim has brought about significant changes in the organisations that she has worked with. A strong advocate of capability building, she is pivotal in crystallising the shape of the future IT workforce. She demonstrated vision and boldness with her push for the early incubation of in-house User Experience Design capabilities in 2010 and Agile Software Development capabilities in 2012 when she was the CIO at Ministry of Manpower. In March 2020 she was the Mission Lead for Workforce Transformation for 1,200 GovTech staff to uplift their digital competencies in agile, data, design thinking and cloud.

She was Vice President of the Executive Council of IT Management Association (ITMA) from 2014-2017. Mui Kim has been chairing the Singapore Computer Society’s (SCS) Board of Assessor for “Certification in Outsourcing Management for IT” (COMIT) since 2012. She was conferred a fellowship by the SCS in 2016 for her contributions in the IT industry.

Mr Bruce Liang
Board Director
Mr. Bruce Liang is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Integrated Health Information Systems (IHiS) Pte Ltd since Nov 2016. Mr Liang also held concurrent appointments as the Chief Information Officer of the Ministry of Health Singapore (MOH) from Feb 2013 to Sept 2020, and the Chief Information Officer of MOH Holdings from Feb 2013 to Oct 2016.

Mr. Liang plays a key role in the advancement of Health Technology in Singapore. He is actively involved in the planning, development, and operations of Health Technology policies, strategies, cyber defence, architectures, standards, digital apps, platforms and systems to enable the dynamic and complex nature of healthcare delivery. Mr Liang is overall responsible for the management of more than 700 technology products that benefit users including MOH, all public healthcare institutions and more than 1,000 private institutions comprising GP Clinics, Community Hospitals, Nursing Homes and more.

Prior to joining MOH and IHiS, Mr. Liang held various leadership roles in Accenture over his 19-year career with the multinational corporation, including Managing Director for Growth and Operations of ASEAN Products Practice as well as the Health and Public Sector Practice in Asia Emerging Markets. He has extensive experience in complex and large scale enterprise planning and Business-IT transformation programs for Governments and large businesses across Asia.

Mr. Liang is an alumni of the National University of Singapore (NUS) School of Computing and is actively involved in the development of the infocomm industry. He serves and advises on the Executive Committee of Singapore Computer Society (SCS), IMDA’s Governing Council of TechSkills Accelerator (TeSA), NUS School of Computing Industry Advisory Committee (IAC), Advisory Board of the NTU Ageing Research Institute for Society and Education (ARISE) and LccL Coding Academy.
