BUILDING
ON TRUST

At Assurity, we empower you for the digital economy by securing the trust your customers place in their online transactions with your business.

Each end user is issued a certificate by the National Certification Authority, which is used together with Singpass to facilitate seamless and safe transactions for consumers and businesses.

Today, we are responsible for driving adoption of the full suite of Singpass API products (Myinfo, Login, Verify, Sign, Identiface, and Authorise) across the government and private sectors.

CASE STUDIES
Ray of Hope is a crowdfunding charity that has implemented Myinfo to verify beneficiaries, and Login to verify campaigners who start campaigns on behalf of others.
Netrust has implemented Login and Sign with Singpass to empower businesses and their customers to login seamlessly, and digitally sign any document using their Singpass app.
Singaporeans, Permanent Residents and Singapore Work Pass Holders with active individual and joint CDP securities accounts can access their CDP securities portfolios, account statements, and notifications using Singpass.
